# Baseline 117.43


## 0. 成功配置

$$
\text{run}=(\text{MSVD},\ ks12,\ bs=8,\ epochs=20,\ scheduler=\text{cosine},\ warmup=5,\ beam=5,\ \alpha=0.7,\ lr=5\times10^{-4},\ min\_lr=10^{-6})
$$

$$
\text{CIDEr}=117.43\quad (\text{epoch }19)
$$

## 1. 记号

$$
B=\text{batch},\quad T=\text{video length},\quad D=512
$$

$$
L=77,\quad L'=76,\quad V=49408
$$

## 2. 数据集输出

$$
X_v \in \mathbb{R}^{T\times 512},\quad m_v\in\{0,1\}^{T},\quad c\in\mathbb{Z}^{77},\quad m_c\in\{0,1\}^{77}
$$

$$
X_v \in \mathbb{R}^{B\times T\times 512},\quad m_v\in\{0,1\}^{B\times T},\quad c\in\mathbb{Z}^{B\times 77},\quad m_c\in\{0,1\}^{B\times 77}
$$

## 3. Teacher Forcing

$$
c_{in}=c_{[:,:L-1]}\in\mathbb{Z}^{B\times 76},\quad y=c_{[:,1:]}\in\mathbb{Z}^{B\times 76}
$$

$$
m_{in}=m_{c,[:,:L-1]}\in\{0,1\}^{B\times 76}
$$

## 4. 视频侧

$$
M=\operatorname{MeanPool}(X_v,m_v)\in\mathbb{R}^{B\times 1\times 512}
$$

$$
M_b=\frac{\sum_{t=1}^{T} m_{b,t}X_{b,t}}{\max\left(\sum_{t=1}^{T} m_{b,t},1\right)}
$$

$$
[B,T,512]\rightarrow[B,1,512]
$$

## 5. 文本侧

$$
E_{tok}=\operatorname{Emb}(c_{in})\in\mathbb{R}^{B\times 76\times 512}
$$

$$
E_{txt}=\operatorname{LN}(\operatorname{Pos}(E_{tok}))\in\mathbb{R}^{B\times 76\times 512}
$$

$$
[B,76]\rightarrow[B,76,512]
$$

## 6. 解码器

$$
H=\operatorname{Decoder}(E_{txt},M)\in\mathbb{R}^{B\times 76\times 512}
$$

## 7. 词表投影

$$
Z=\operatorname{LMHead}(H)\in\mathbb{R}^{B\times 76\times 49408}
$$

$$
[B,76,512]\rightarrow[B,76,49408]
$$

## 8. 损失

$$
Z\rightarrow\mathbb{R}^{(B\cdot 76)\times 49408},\quad y\rightarrow\mathbb{Z}^{B\cdot 76}
$$

$$
\mathcal{L}_{CE}=\operatorname{CE}(Z,y;\ \text{ignore\_index}=0)
$$

## 9. 训练主链路

$$
X_v[B,T,512],\ m_v[B,T],\ c[B,77],\ m_c[B,77]
$$

$$
c[B,77]\rightarrow c_{in}[B,76],\ y[B,76]
$$

$$
X_v[B,T,512]\rightarrow M[B,1,512]
$$

$$
c_{in}[B,76]\rightarrow E_{txt}[B,76,512]
$$

$$
(E_{txt}[B,76,512],\ M[B,1,512])\rightarrow H[B,76,512]\rightarrow Z[B,76,49408]
$$

$$
(Z[B,76,49408],\ y[B,76])\rightarrow \mathcal{L}_{CE}
$$

## 10. 推理主链路

$$
s^{(0)}=[\text{BOS}]
$$

$$
c^{(t)}\in\mathbb{Z}^{B\times (t+1)},\quad m^{(t)}\in\{0,1\}^{B\times (t+1)}
$$

$$
Z^{(t)}=f(X_v,m_v,c^{(t)},m^{(t)})\in\mathbb{R}^{B\times t\times 49408}
$$

$$
z^{(t)}=Z^{(t)}_{[:,-1,:]}\in\mathbb{R}^{B\times 49408}
$$

$$
s^{(t+1)}=\arg\max z^{(t)}\quad \text{or}\quad \operatorname{BeamSearch}(z^{(t)};\ beam=5,\ \alpha=0.7)
$$

## 11. 评测

$$
\hat{Y}=\operatorname{Decode}(X_v,m_v)
$$

$$
\operatorname{Metrics}(\hat{Y},Y_{ref})=\{\text{BLEU-4},\ \text{ROUGE-L},\ \text{CIDEr},\ \text{METEOR}\}
$$

## 12. 一行总式

$$
X_v[B,T,512]\xrightarrow{\text{mean pool}}M[B,1,512],\quad c[B,77]\xrightarrow{\text{shift}}c_{in}[B,76],\quad (c_{in},M)\xrightarrow{\text{emb+pos+decoder}}H[B,76,512]\xrightarrow{\text{lm head}}Z[B,76,49408]
$$
